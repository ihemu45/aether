How to play
🎶 THEREMIN
Right hand sets pitch, left hand sets volume. Wave your hands in the air.
🥁 AIR DRUMS
Strike the floating pads with a quick downward motion. Two hands.
🎼 AIR HARP
Sweep across the glowing strings to pluck. Always in key.

CAMERA REQUIRED

Troubleshooting

  • Click the camera icon in your browser's address bar to grant access.
  • Make sure no other app (Zoom, Teams, Photo Booth) is using the webcam.
  • Reload the tab to retry initialization.
